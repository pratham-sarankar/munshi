Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Munshi - your privacy-first personal finance tracker

Yes! Munshi is open source under the MIT license and will always remain free. We believe personal finance tracking should be accessible to everyone without subscription fees or hidden costs. The app generates no revenue and has no ads - it's a passion project built for the community.

All your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't use any cloud services or external servers. Your transactions, categories, and financial information never leave your phone. This ensures complete privacy and means you can use Munshi even without an internet connection.

Absolutely! Munshi is designed to work completely offline. You don't need an internet connection to track expenses, view reports, or use any core features. The app stores everything locally, making it perfect for travel or areas with limited connectivity.

Munshi supports all major world currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, and many more. You can easily switch between currencies or track expenses in multiple currencies simultaneously. The app uses standard currency codes and symbols for consistency.

We welcome contributions! You can help by reporting bugs, suggesting features, contributing code, or improving documentation. Visit our GitHub repository to get started. Even feedback and reviews help the project grow.

Your data is as secure as it gets - it never leaves your device! Since everything is stored locally, there's no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access through internet connections. You control your device's security measures like lock screens and encryption to protect your information.

Yes! Munshi provides export functionality to backup your data. You can export your transactions to CSV or JSON formats and store them wherever you prefer - cloud storage, local files, or email them to yourself. You have complete control over your backup strategy.

Munshi is built with Flutter, so it runs natively on both Android and iOS devices. We're planning to add support for web and desktop platforms in the future. The app is optimized for mobile use with touch-friendly interfaces and responsive design.

You can reach out through multiple channels: email us at support@sarankar.com, open an issue on our GitHub repository, or use the feedback feature within the app itself.

No ads, no tracking, no analytics - period. Munshi doesn't collect any user data or behavior information. There are no third-party services, no cookies, and no hidden telemetry. The app is completely clean and respects your privacy by design, not just by policy.
